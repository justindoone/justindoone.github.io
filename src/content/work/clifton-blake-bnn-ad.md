The Mortgage Fund needed a push leading into RRSP season. We ran a three-month integrated campaign across BNN Bloomberg, radio, and the paid digital channels (Meta, Google, LinkedIn).

BNN was the broadcast anchor. The spot ran throughout the day at specific intervals, on the channel where financial professionals were already paying attention to market commentary. The other channels ran the full three months. I led the campaign with the SVP of Investor Relations.

The first month of digital alone pulled in roughly 250,000 impressions at a cost per click under $2. BNN's job in the mix was credibility, sitting in the slot where a fund's institutional respectability gets measured in Canada. The digital channels handled the volume side, where the metrics could land cleanly. Radio caught the commuter and podcast audiences that fell outside the digital reach.
