Inside Track is Greybrook Realty Partners' annual invite-only investor event. Roughly 400 in the room in October 2025. I produced it and ran the content program around the day.

The work ran in three phases. The pre-event window covered save-the-date emails to the invite list and a teaser piece a couple of weeks before the day. On the day itself I managed content capture: the panels, audience reactions, and side conversations with guests and staff. Post-event ran for the two to three weeks after, distributing edited highlights and recorded interviews across email, the blog, the firm's site, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

The room landed on the day. The post-event content stretch was the part most easily underestimated, since five surfaces of distribution off a single recording day takes more sequencing work than the live event itself.
