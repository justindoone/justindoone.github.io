Roxborough Communities is Clifton Blake's purpose-built-rental brand. Cricket Park, College West, and a handful of others sit under it, across a mix of residential and mixed-use buildings. The brand had had a website once. By the time we picked it up it had been taken down, so this was effectively a fresh launch.

I owned the marketing side for the residential tenant audience and worked with an agency on the build. The commercial portfolio was managed separately.

The site had two jobs to do at once. It had to function as the brand surface for the whole Roxborough portfolio that prospective tenants would land on when researching any of the buildings. And it had to act as the lead funnel for each individual property, with the Roxborough page linking out to the building-specific site that ran its own social, paid campaigns, and tour-booking flow.

The model leaned hard on direct-to-tenant marketing. Per-property paid campaigns were doing most of the lift on any given building. Most leases came through our own channels, with agents handling a small share of the volume.

Properties under my tenure hit 90%+ occupancy within six months of launch, with marketing carrying the funnel up to the in-person showing and leasing closing from there.
