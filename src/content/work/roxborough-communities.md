Roxborough Communities is Clifton Blake's residential property management arm. Cricket Park, College West, and a handful of others sit under it, across a mix of residential and mixed-use buildings. The brand had had a website once. By the time we picked it up it had been taken down, so this was effectively a fresh launch.

I owned the marketing side for the residential tenant audience and worked with an agency on the build. The commercial portfolio was managed separately.

The site had to do two jobs at once. It had to function as a brand surface for the whole Roxborough portfolio, which prospective tenants would land on when researching any of the buildings under the brand. And it had to act as the lead funnel for each individual property's lease-up campaign, where the campaign creative pointed people at the right building-specific page on the site for tour booking.

Roxborough properties launch into 90%+ occupancy within six months. The site was a necessary piece of that, but the heavier lift on any individual lease-up came from the campaign work for each building.
